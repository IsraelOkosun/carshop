What Israel's Auto Shop is all about.

Israel Auto shop is an internationally recognized car services garage that deals with the repair, servicing of different types of automobiles. We started with just one garage shop in 1995 but as time passed we had to expand to more than 25 garages due to the number of customers we were recieving. Each garage managed by Israel's Auto shop is highly equipped with the lastest and state of the art machinery for repairs, servicing, painting, installation, rewiring, etc. We always want our customers to be satified to the fullest wit our services. Twice Every year we give discounts on car painting(deppending on the color), rewiring and installions. This enables us to advertise and gain more customers thereby increasing our coast and reach

Car Repair

We repair different over 30 car brands according to their issues ranging from oil leakage, engine failure, air conditioning issue, exhaust rusting, wheel bearing problems, fan belt replacement, head lights replacement, glass replacment, wheel balancing. Due to our partnership with some car brands we give discounts on some types of repairs.s

Wheel Alignment

Israel's Auto shop has noticed a common issue with newly released car brands and that issue is that of the wheel alignmet of the cars. So we have dedicated a special section handle this constant problem with the cars. Our new and improved machinery are highly capable of solving the wheel alignmetn issue. Our customers success stories are our testimonies.

Car Wash

Our cash was is an addition and an edge over other automobile shops. We offer a 24hour cash wash at all our branches. We noticed that when cars leave our auto shop they are sometimes dirty so based on the fact that they repaired with us they get a free wash but other peple that just come for the cash wash alone would have to pay for the car wash service.
